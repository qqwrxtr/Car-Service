Our Journey Began With A Simple Belief

Every car owner deserves honest, professional service without the runaround. No upselling, no surprises, just skilled technicians who care about your safety and your wallet.

The Beginning

From Passion to Profession

It started in Michael's garage in 2010. Friends and neighbors kept asking for help with their cars because they trusted his work and fair pricing. Word spread quickly through Woolloomooloo.

What began as weekend favor became a calling. Michael realized the community needed a different kind of auto shop – one that treated customers like family and vehicles like they were our own.

Today, Domain stands as proof that doing right by people never goes out of style. We've grown, but our values remain the same.
