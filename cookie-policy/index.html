What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device when you visit them. They help websites remember information about your visit, making your experience more convenient and personalized.

At Domain, we use cookies responsibly to enhance your browsing experience, understand how you interact with our website, and provide you with relevant information about our automotive services. We believe in transparency about how and why we use cookies.

Most websites use cookie consent banners or preference centers to allow you to control cookie settings. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings as described in the "Managing Cookies" section below.

Managing Cookies

Browser Cookie Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

Chrome

  1. 1. Click the three dots menu
  2. 2. Go to Settings > Privacy and security
  3. 3. Click "Cookies and other site data"
  4. 4. Choose your preferred settings

Firefox

  1. 1. Click the menu button
  2. 2. Select Settings
  3. 3. Go to Privacy & Security
  4. 4. Adjust "Cookies and Site Data"

Safari

  1. 1. Click Safari menu
  2. 2. Choose Preferences
  3. 3. Click Privacy tab
  4. 4. Manage cookie settings

Edge

  1. 1. Click the three dots menu
  2. 2. Go to Settings
  3. 3. Select Privacy, search, and services
  4. 4. Manage "Cookies and site permissions"

Opera

  1. 1. Click the Opera menu
  2. 2. Go to Settings
  3. 3. Click Privacy & security
  4. 4. Adjust "Cookies and other site data"

Mobile

Mobile browsers have similar settings in their respective settings menus. Look for "Privacy" or "Site Settings" options.

Common Cookie Questions

How do I completely disable cookies?

You can disable all cookies through your browser settings, but this may significantly impact website functionality. We recommend using our cookie preference center for granular control.

Why do I still see the cookie banner after accepting?

This may happen if cookies are being blocked by your browser, you're in private/incognito mode, or if you've cleared your cookies. The banner will reappear until preferences are successfully saved.

Do you share cookie data with other companies?

We only share anonymized, aggregated data with trusted partners for analytics and service improvement. We never sell personal data or detailed browsing information to third parties.

Can I request all my cookie data?

Yes, under Australian Privacy Principles, you can request access to any personal information we hold, including data collected through cookies. Contact us to submit a data access request.

Privacy Rights

You have the right to know what cookies we use, why we use them, and how to control them. If you believe we're not handling your cookie preferences correctly, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) or NSW Fair Trading.
