Privacy Overview

At Domain (Eastern Suburbs Automotive), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us in person at our Woolloomooloo location.

We understand that your privacy is important to you, and we want you to feel confident about how we handle your personal information. This policy applies to all information collected by Domain through our website, mobile applications, email communications, and in-person interactions at our facility.

Our Commitment to You

  • We collect only the information necessary to provide our services
  • We never sell or rent your personal information to third parties
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • We provide clear choices about how your information is used

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • • Schedule service appointments
  • • Request quotes or estimates
  • • Create an account on our website
  • • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • • Contact us via phone, email, or contact forms
  • • Visit our facility in person

This may include: Name, email address, phone number, postal address, vehicle information (make, model, year, VIN), service history, and payment information.

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • • IP address and browser type
  • • Device information (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • • Referring website or search terms
  • • Date and time of visits
  • • Geographic location (general area)

Communication Records

We keep records of our communications with you, including:

  • • Phone call logs and voicemails
  • • Email correspondence
  • • Text message exchanges
  • • Service appointment notes
  • • Customer feedback and reviews

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better user experience and understand how our website is used.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality, such as security and navigation

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website to improve performance

Functional Cookies

Remember your preferences and settings to enhance your experience

Marketing Cookies

Used to provide relevant content and measure advertising effectiveness (with your consent)

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings or our cookie preference center:

  • • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies
  • • Cookie banner: Accept or decline non-essential cookies when you first visit
  • • Preference center: Update your choices at any time through our settings

Note: Blocking essential cookies may affect website functionality. For detailed instructions on managing cookies, visit our Cookie Policy.

Children's Privacy

Age Requirement

Our services are not intended for children under 18 years of age.

Domain does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 years of age. Our website and services are designed for adults who are seeking automotive repair and maintenance services.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information from our records as quickly as possible.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at (02) 9662 0423 or through our contact form.
